Our Legacy

For over 50 years, Kondaji Chivda has been perfecting the art of traditional Maharashtrian snack-making. Born in the fertile fields of Nashik, our chivda carries authentic flavors passed down through three generations of chivda karigars.

What Makes Kondaji Chivda Unique?

Traditional Bhaaji Technique: Slow-roasted in copper kadhai over wood fire

• Monsoon Special: Aged in neem wood containers for anti-humidity crispness

• Health Conscious: No palm oil (uses groundnut + sesame blend) Jaggery instead of refined sugar Gluten-free & vegan options available
